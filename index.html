One of my favorite ways to spend my free time is by rewatching the Star Wars films—especially the original and prequel trilogies. There’s something comforting about returning to those familiar characters, iconic moments, and sweeping stories of adventure and hope. While I haven’t explored much of the expanded universe through the many Star Wars books just yet, I’m eager to dive deeper into that side of the galaxy in the future and experience the stories that go beyond the films.

I also enjoy playing a wide variety of video games, each offering a different kind of world to explore. Some of my favorites include Red Dead Redemption 2, with its rich storytelling and breathtaking landscapes; Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order, which lets me step into the boots of a Jedi in a galaxy far, far away; and Fallout 4, with its mix of post-apocalyptic survival and player-driven choice. Whether it’s through film, books, or games, I love immersing myself in engaging stories and well-crafted worlds.
